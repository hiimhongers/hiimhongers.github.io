Innovative Design


InnoD is Cal's student-run creative agency, and does graphic design, photography, videography and web design for on and off campus clients.

During my time in the club, I led three video teams, served as treasurer for a year, and planned a bunch of internal events as VP of internal affairs.

I've been in the club since August 2014, so it's been a long ride.



This is our logo. I've got a hat embroidered with it, vinyl stickers with it and a jacket with it, which I've been told I wear way too often.


Work

Being the resident 'video and motion graphics' guy, I produced a lot of graphics and video for club events like recruitment and our annual designathons/workshop days. Typically it involved animating flyers that were already made in Illustrator or coming up with animations of my own.


A looping promo video/gif I made for Fall 2017 recruitment.
 
Simple shapes and nicely eased animation - those were the two goals I hoped to achieve with any motion work I did for the club.

For a year, the animations I made were the splashes on our website. Pretty neat!

 
I led three teams of 10 videographers, one each semester.
