Gumnut


We're developing a delightful productivity suite.

While working on a finance application, my team became a bit frustrated with how we were communicating and collaborating. Files shared between Dropbox, Google Drive, Slack, tasks on Trello, Asana, appointments on Calendly... it seemed like a no-brainer that they should've been integrated.

In the dearth of delightful calendar apps following the demise of Sunrise, we decided to start work on a calendar that could bring all of your other applications together to make your day a little bit less cluttered.

We're working hard on building out the app and we have a working private demo. E-mail me for more information.
